🖱️🖱️🐛 Double click for debug inspect (bookmarklet)

1) Open devtools, 2) invoke bookmarklet, 3) double click on pesky ephemeral element, 4) JS processing is paused and clicked element logged in console for further inspection.

Thanks @leahjlou for idea!

In Firefox you can disable debugger overlay with devtools.debugger.features.overlay false about:config pref (thans all involved in bug 1579768.
